The beauty and wellness industry in Tamil Nadu is witnessing an unprecedented boom. With rising disposable incomes and a growing consciousness about grooming, salon franchises have become one of the most lucrative business opportunities in the state. From premium luxury lounges to affordable neighborhood cuts, the market is thriving.

If you are looking to invest in a salon franchise, knowing the market leaders is crucial. Here is a comprehensive list of the most prominent salon franchises in Tamil Nadu, ranked by reach and popularity.

2. Naturals Salon

A household name in India, Naturals has been a pioneer in the salon franchise ecosystem. Known for its focus on women empowerment, Naturals has a strong presence in Chennai and other major cities.

  • Focus: Unisex & Premium services
  • Strength: Strong training academy and long-standing reputation.

3. Green Trends

Owned by CavinKare, Green Trends is another major player offering professional grooming services at affordable prices. They are known for their standardized service quality and family-friendly environment.

  • Focus: Family salon services
  • Strength: Corporate backing and organized operations.

4. Jawed Habib Hair & Beauty

Bringing the celebrity hairstylist touch to the masses, Jawed Habib is famous for hair education and styling. It attracts a young and trendy crowd looking for expert haircuts.

5. Toni & Guy

For those looking for ultra-premium and international styling standards, Toni & Guy is the go-to brand. Their franchises are typically located in high-end neighborhoods and malls.
