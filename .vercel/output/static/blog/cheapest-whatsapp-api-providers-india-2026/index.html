WhatsApp Business API has become one of the most powerful tools for businesses in India to connect with customers. But with so many providers offering different pricing, features, and support levels, choosing the right one can be overwhelming. In this guide, we compare the 5 cheapest WhatsApp API providers in India — breaking down their pricing, setup fees, markup charges, user limits, and key features so you can make an informed decision.

1. Wapi.in — Best Budget Starter Plan

Wapi.in offers one of the lowest entry-point plans in the Indian WhatsApp API market. Starting at just ₹599/month, it is ideal for small businesses or solo entrepreneurs who are just getting started with WhatsApp automation. However, note that the plan comes with a one-time setup fee of ₹7,000 and a per-message markup of ₹0.15, so costs can add up as your message volume grows.

Key Features

  • 5,000 Contacts & 30 Campaigns per month on the Premium plan
  • AI Chatbot with up to 100 bot replies and 10 bot flows
  • API & Webhook Access for custom integrations
  • 2 Team Members/Agents — suitable for small support teams
  • WhatsApp Common Inbox with broadcast & campaign messaging

2. Cleomitra — Best Value for Unlimited Users

Cleomitra is an excellent choice for growing teams and agencies. At ₹999/month, you get unlimited users, unlimited messages, zero setup fees, and zero markup on messages. This makes it one of the most cost-effective options in India, especially for businesses with large teams or high message volumes.

Key Features

  • Unlimited users and messages with no extra charges
  • AI Chatbot & Auto-reply for 24/7 engagement
  • Zero setup fee — get started immediately without upfront cost
  • Zero markup on WhatsApp messages — pay only Meta's official rates
  • CRM Dashboard for managing customer conversations
  • Broadcast & campaign messaging with contact management

3. WABAConnect — Best for Enterprises & Analytics

WABAConnect is a feature-rich platform suited for enterprises and large-scale operations. At ₹999/month, it offers unlimited messages and unlimited users — making it a strong competitor to Cleomitra. What sets WABAConnect apart is its advanced analytics, agent management, and growth consultation services.

Key Features

  • Bulk Broadcasting with team shared inbox for collaborative support
  • ChatBots with developer API access for custom automation
  • Agent & Organisation Analytics with detailed reports
  • Green Tick Verification support to build brand trust
  • Growth consultation and special customizations for enterprise needs

4. Bot.Space — Best for AI-Powered Automation

Bot.Space is designed for AI-first businesses that want to automate customer interactions at scale. At ₹999/month with 5 seats, it includes powerful AI agents, AI copilots, and AI-driven reporting — making it unique in this price range. The ₹0.15/message markup is a consideration for high-volume users.

Key Features

  • AI Agent & AI Copilot for intelligent automated customer support
  • Up to 10 workflows for multi-step automation sequences
  • Unlimited contacts with lead management and smart agent routing
  • AI-powered reporting and conversation analytics
  • Campaign broadcasting with macros and multi-keyword support

5. Bol7 — Best for High-Volume Broadcasters

Bol7 is a well-established WhatsApp API provider in India that offers unlimited messages and unlimited users at ₹1,000/month — with zero markup on messages. While the one-time setup fee of ₹4,000 applies, the zero-markup model makes it cost-effective for businesses with very high outbound message volumes like e-commerce, logistics, and retail.

Key Features

  • Unlimited messages and users with no per-message markup
  • Broadcast campaigns for mass outreach to large customer bases
  • Smart agent routing to ensure conversations reach the right team
  • Contact attributes and conversation analytics for data-driven decisions
  • Priority support with notes, reminders, and webhooks/Reply Message API

Understanding Meta's Official WhatsApp Charges

All WhatsApp Business API providers are required to pass on Meta's official per-conversation charges. These are standardized and apply regardless of which provider you choose:

Conversation TypeMeta Charge (per msg)Who Pays
Utility / Auth₹0.115 – ₹0.145Business
Marketing₹0.86 – ₹1.09Business
User-initiated (Service)Free—

Providers with zero markup (Cleomitra, WABAConnect, Bol7) only charge you Meta's rates. Providers with a markup (Wapi.in, Bot.Space at ₹0.15/msg) add on top of these charges.

Which Provider Should You Choose?

The right WhatsApp API provider depends on your business size, message volume, and budget. Here is a quick recommendation guide:

  • Just starting out, low budget: Wapi.in (Lowest monthly cost at ₹599)
  • Growing team, many agents: Cleomitra (Unlimited users, zero markup)
  • Large enterprise, need analytics: WABAConnect (Unlimited + advanced reporting)
  • AI automation is a priority: Bot.Space (AI agent + copilot features)
  • High-volume bulk messaging: Bol7 (Unlimited msgs, zero markup)

Conclusion

India's WhatsApp API market is growing rapidly and there are now several affordable, feature-rich providers to choose from. Whether you are a startup looking for a low-cost entry point or an enterprise needing unlimited scale and AI automation, there is a provider on this list that fits your needs.
